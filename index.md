# Jen Anderson #

## About ##
I am an information management professional working in library acquisitions. 

In my daily work, I see that effective collection development decisions are only as good as the underpinning data. When records are incomplete or inconsistent, informed decision-making becomes impossible. I am interested in building tools and processes to bridge knowledge gaps and ensure that library collections meet the needs of current and future users.

## Professional focus ##
- Acquisitions and collection development: Managing the complex data lifecycle required to create and maintain relevant collections.
- Data integrity: Applying technical rigour to metadata and records management to maintain transparency and improve understanding of both collection use and user needs.
- Process improvement: Creating lightweight, efficient tools to solve information bottlenecks.

I maintain this site as a record of coding projects and explorations. It reflects my interest in pragmatic and targeted solutions, as well as the potential of connected data.

## Projects ##
- [Serials analytics](./projects/serials-analytics/README.md): Automating demographic and collection circulation data to improve purchasing and allocation decisions.
- [Solvent substitution](./projects/solvent-substitution/README): A structured decision-making tool to predict solvent effectiveness, manage compliance and mitigate risks.
- [Library search](./projects/library-search/README.md): Optimising metadata for improved user experience searching catalogue and creating book records.
